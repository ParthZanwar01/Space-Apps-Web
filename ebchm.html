Electron-Beam Cold-Hearth Melting

Advanced Metal Refining Technology

Purpose

Used to refine and purify mixed-metal debris collected from orbit into uniform, high-quality alloys suitable for manufacturing.

How It Works

1

Electron Beam Generation

An electron gun emits a focused beam in vacuum; debris struck by electrons melt.

2

Cold-Hearth Design

Molten metal sits in water-cooled copper hearth, preventing contamination.

3

Refinement Process

Dense inclusions sink, lighter impurities float as slag, cleaned metal overflow forms ingots.

4

Vacuum Processing

Entire process in vacuum to avoid oxidation/hydrogen absorption.
